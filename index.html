Welcome

CMS SCHOOL

  Senior Secondary CMS Central School Calicut, affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi
  is a coveted center of learning located on the bank of the Canoli Canal. 
  The School is a privately owned, managed and funded institution belonging 
  to the CMS State committee.

  This institution brings to bear a focus not only on academic excellence, 
  but also cultural, emotional and spiritual empowerment of youth. The 
  school offers a challenging curriculum and an enriching learning 
  experience from Pre-Primary Montessori to Grade XII.
